## 🐛 How to Report Issues

### Found a Bug?
1. **Take a screenshot** if possible
2. **Note what you were doing** when it happened
3. **Send feedback to:** testing@pandamart.co.ke
4. **Include:**
   - Your device (iPhone, Android, Computer)
   - Browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)
   - What you expected vs what happened

### Feedback Categories
- 🐛 **Bug:** Something broken or not working
- 💡 **Suggestion:** Ideas for improvement
- 😕 **Confusing:** Something unclear or hard to use
- 👍 **Great:** Something you really liked
